Our Philosophy

Dining as an Art Form

At Kings & Queens, we believe that dining is more than just eating — it's an experience that engages all the senses. Every element of our restaurant, from the carefully curated ambiance to the meticulously crafted dishes, is designed to make you feel like royalty.

Our philosophy is rooted in three pillars: exceptional quality, authentic hospitality, and continuous innovation. We source only the finest ingredients, train our staff to anticipate your every need, and constantly evolve our menu to surprise and delight.

The Beginning

A Dream Realized

Kings & Queens was born from a simple yet ambitious dream: to create a dining destination where every guest feels like royalty. Founded in 2020 by Chef Ada Okonkwo, our restaurant started as a small 20-seat eatery with big aspirations.

Within just two years, word spread throughout Calabar about our exceptional cuisine and unmatched hospitality. We expanded to our current location — a stunning 200-seat venue with a dedicated lounge, private dining rooms, and an outdoor terrace.

Today, Kings & Queens is recognized as one of the finest restaurants in Cross River State, having won "Best Restaurant" awards in 2024 and 2025. But our greatest achievement? The smiles on our guests' faces and the memories they create at our tables.
