FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, we welcome walk-ins! However, we recommend making a reservation, especially for weekends and special occasions, to ensure availability.

Is there a dress code?

We maintain a smart casual dress code. We kindly ask guests to avoid beachwear, flip-flops, and overly casual attire.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! Our chefs can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary needs. Please inform us when making your reservation.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have complimentary valet parking for all our guests, as well as a secure parking lot adjacent to the restaurant.
